If it's useful to copy the metadata database into git, you can use SQLite to visualize changes. It's not perfect but works in a pinch and probably better than you might think:

$ tee -a .gitattributes
*.db       diff=sqlite3

$ tee -a .git/config
[diff "sqlite3"]
    binary = true
    textconv = "echo .dump | sqlite3"
