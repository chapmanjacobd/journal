This site is a bit gimmicky but also a bit fun: https://curaturae.com/faq

Last year [2021], the Smithsonian developed Open Access, a dataset with over 3 million objects from their collections for public use on the internet. Using today’s technology we can bring these objects to you in surprising ways.

After you write something, the application responds with images from Open Access.

Sound good? Then let’s go.

This project supports writing in Chinese, English, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, and Spanish.
