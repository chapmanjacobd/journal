I came across this really cool site via eat me, by Alice Yuan Zhang. The format is imperfect but really neat!

SPECTER, by kevin cunanan chappelle for example, has a bunch of rotating things and it is hard to read the text if you only hover over things instead of clicking. At first, it seems like a jumbled mess but I think that plays well into the idea of memories, emotions, and reminiscing the past.

Where We Go From Here, by Delta_Ark feels a lot more like the Metaverse than any virtual reality headset ever did.

There's a lot more. Highly recommend this site
