WHY DO I HAVE THIS ON MY COMPUTER ??!

I can appreciate that it outputs 7.1 terawatts (at cruising speed) but I don’t need it in my life anymore.

I recently wrote a little script to help identify easy wins for deleting unneeded files and organizing my computer. It’s available here as part of my free CLI program, library .

It’s a simple script but the results have been helpful.

pip install xklb

library xr --filesystem fs.db /mnt/d/8*

library bigdirs fs.db

And now it’s gone. 109 GB freed up. Bliss.
