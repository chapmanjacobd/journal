I've been listening to the Space Adventure Cobra (1982) OST.

Composed by Kentarô Haneda and Yûji Ôno, it is a vibrant fusion of '80s synth, jazz, and cosmic swagger. Sweeping orchestral arrangements, rich with dramatic flair, intertwine seamlessly with jazz grooves and futuristic electronic beats.

It reminds me a bit of the Fantastic Planet (1973) OST.
