I recently discovered Ariel Kalma. Wow! Big catalog covering a wide variety of genres.

I'll start you off with something familiar but saxophone: Moonlight Sonata.

If you liked that maybe try his Moonlight Suite:

In 1983 I moved from Paris to Hamburg to live with my sweetheart. I bought a 4 track ¼” tape recorder and composed Moonlight as a suite of vignettes illustrating Shamballa, the land of silence and bliss. Nightingale Records needed a new age version (Rainbow), so I added birds to the mix when they published a cassette (I was not satisfied with the sound quality). Then I left for Hawaii, raised a son and did not remix and remaster until… 2018. This time (almost) no birds.

Or perhaps you'd like something more modern? Or something more raw? New Age?
