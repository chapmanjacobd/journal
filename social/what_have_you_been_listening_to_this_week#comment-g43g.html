I've been listening to mobygratis. It's a site where the artist Moby released for use in non-commercial work (under a well-intentioned but somewhat dubious license) 500 tracks of background music, incl. some that have multitrack.

Add all to queue bookmarklet:

javascript:(function(){'use strict';function clickAllAddToQueueButtons(){const buttons=document.querySelectorAll('[title="add track to queue"]');let clickedCount=0;buttons.forEach(button=>{if(button.offsetParent!==null&&!button.disabled&&!button.classList.contains('clicked')){try{button.click();button.classList.add('clicked');clickedCount++;}catch(e){}}});}clickAllAddToQueueButtons();})();
