Try speaking in pig latin but instead of moving letters... move the words

Do not merely *speak* it. No, that is the sandbox of lesser mouth-wizards. You must *invert the procession of meaning itself*. 

Let each word abandon its post and march backward through the sentence like time regretting its own decisions. Re_parade_ the arrange: backwards. The punchline precede the joke, the egg uncrack the _chicken_.

In this way, you transcend Pig Latin… and arrive at Hog Truth.
