One Million Screenshots is an interactive Leaflet map which allows you to pan and zoom around the screenshots of the top 1 million websites. To make the map the company Urlbox took screenshots of the top 1,048,576 ranked websites by Common Crawl Web Graph.

Browsing One Million Screenshots makes me think about how inconsequential I am... which is a good perspective to have sometimes... I think it is a similar feeling of mild dissociation to the "overview effect"
