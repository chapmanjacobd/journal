People may disagree, but this is what I understand modes and scales to be:

Scales are sets of intervals. Modes are arrangements of scales and they have an explicit starting interval and order. Scales have order, but no start or end.

Greek Scale: \[1\]WWHWWWH. This is still the greek scale: \[5\]WWHWWHW, but this is not the greek scale: WWWHWHW (it's not just from a different starting point, it's a different order)

The Ionian mode of the Greek scale is explicitly **WWHWWWH**.
